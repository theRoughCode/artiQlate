ArtiQlate: Create quantum circuits with your voice!

Start creating a circuit by clicking on the "Start Command" button below and saying "Create a circuit with 3 qubits". You should see a circuit with 3 qubits generated on-screen.

You can then apply gates such as the X gate by hitting the "Start Command" button again and saying "Apply X gate to qubit 1". To measure a qubit, simply say "Measure qubit 2".

Have fun learning about quantum algorithms!
